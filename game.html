Help 1. You can change the population using your cursor: Clicking existing tiles to remove that population or empty ones to add. 2. You can use keys to change the species to add. 3. Moving cursor on one tile and its related information of that population will be displayed at right. a. Health indicates if the populations is in good condition. It will decrease as time pass but can be increased by eating. b. Death rate is impacted by the health value, meaning how likely a creature may die in that population. 4. The game ends when all creatures die or you can terminate it by clicking the end to go back to the main page. 5. If you don't want to wait, click "Skip" to see how long the ecosystem last. However, if you choose to skip, you can no longer modify the populations. 6. To see the graph of populations, click on the button at the bottom of the page.
